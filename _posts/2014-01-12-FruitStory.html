The Idea

The assignment for this project was to change an existing behavior for a client... and we were encouraged to use methods and things that may not exist just yet. So this poses a practicality challenge. Clearly, this app isn't possible right now, BUT -- maybe some day in the future it will be. We already are beginning to see phones with projectors, so why not?

Meet Your Hosts

  • Mark the Worm
