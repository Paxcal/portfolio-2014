We went out in the wild -- we watched human behavior, took notes, and drew conclusions. We surveryed over 100 people ages 18-52 to learn about their music and entertainment prefernces, and a few conclusions were drawn:

73%

Listened to music for over 7 hours a week

61%

Also used social media for at least that long.

92%

Listen to music or audio on their phones during their commute

“To design an easy-to-use interface, pay attention to what users do, not what they say. Self-reported claims are unreliable, as are user speculations about future behavior.” – Jakob Nielsen

So, What Does it Do?

This app provides an entirely new creation process. Artists provide music samples or graphics (t-shirt choices, band posters, etc.) to directly recieve insight from fans. The entire process is driven by the power of communication, thus increasing its popularity and 'amping it up' for release time. It's instant A/B testing -- while making fans feel like a part of the band.

It's all about collaboration & discovery.
